micro:bit Playground

A browser-based BLE control panel for the BBC micro:bit V2. Sensors, servos, live graphs, 3D models, and a GamePad — all from Chrome, with zero install.

Open Playground →

What you need

  • A BBC micro:bit V2 (V1 works but has no sound sensor)
  • A USB cable (to flash the firmware once) or batteries
  • Chrome or Edge desktop — or Chrome on Android
  • Bluetooth enabled on your computer
  • Optional: a servo on P1/P2, speakers for the buzzer, a robot buggy chassis

Your first session in 5 steps

1

Flash the firmware (once)

Open makecode.microbit.org → New Project → click {} JavaScript → delete everything → paste the contents of makecode.ts → click Download → drag the .hex onto the MICROBIT drive. When the board shows an X, it's broadcasting BLE and ready.

2

Open the app

Double-click index.html from your download — it opens in your default browser. If that's Chrome or Edge, you're set. First-time visitors see a welcome overlay walking through the basics; press Esc to dismiss.

3

Pick your theme and language

Header → paint-roller dropdown to choose 🌙 Stealth · ⚡ Neon · ☁️ Arctic · 🔥 Blaze. Header → flag dropdown for English · French · Arabic (with full RTL layout). Both preferences persist across reloads.

4

Pair over Bluetooth

Click the big 🔗 Connect to micro:bit button. Pick your board from the browser's Bluetooth picker (named BBC micro:bit [xxxxx]). The status chip turns green — you're in. The firmware replies with HELLO and telemetry starts streaming immediately.

5

Try each tab

Use 1–8 to jump between tabs. One minute per tab is a great first tour:

🎛

Controls

Draw on the 5×5 LED grid, send a heart, try the buzzer

👀

Sensors

Tilt the board, clap, cover the light sensor

⚙️

Motors

Plug a servo into P1, drag the slider

🎮

GamePad

D-pad arrows scroll on the LED display

📈

Graph

Click 🎲 Simulate for instant demo data

🎲

3D Board

Tilt the micro:bit, watch the 3D model mirror you

Want to use the Graph like a lab?

Graph tab → click ⏺ Record, play with the board, click ⏹ Stop. Then:

  • ▶ Replay — play back in real time on an empty chart
  • 💾 Save Session — download as JSON
  • 📷 PNG / 📄 CSV — export chart or spreadsheet data
  • 📝 Note — drop a timestamped annotation on the chart

Send custom data from MakeCode with bluetooth.uartWriteLine("GRAPH:Distance:" + d) — the browser auto-creates a colored line for each label.

Want to try the 3D models?

Tab 6 → 5 interactive models driven by live sensor data:

  • 🎲 micro:bit V2 — LEDs, tilt, buttons, pins, logo, temperature tint
  • 🚗 Robot Buggy — wheels, steering (Servo 1), headlights
  • 🦾 Robot Arm — Servo 1 = base, Servo 2 = lift, buttons = gripper
  • 🎯 Balance Game — tilt the board to roll a ball to targets
  • 🌦️ Weather Station — thermometer, sun/cloud, wind vane, sound bars

Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, toggle Auto Rotate for a hands-free demo.
